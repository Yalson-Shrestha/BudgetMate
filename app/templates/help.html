Help Center

Find answers to common questions about using BudgetMate

Getting Started

Welcome to BudgetMate! Here's how to get started with managing your finances:

  • Sign up for a new account or log in to your existing account
  • Add your first transaction using the "Add Transaction" form on the dashboard
  • View your daily and monthly summaries in the dashboard
  • Check your spending patterns in the Budget Analysis section

Adding Transactions

To add a new transaction:

  • Go to the dashboard
  • Fill in the transaction form with the amount, category, and type (income/expense)
  • Add an optional note for additional details
  • Click the plus button to save the transaction

Understanding Your Budget

Your budget is automatically calculated based on your income and expenses:

  • Monthly Income: Total of all income transactions in the current month
  • Monthly Expenses: Total of all expense transactions in the current month
  • Balance: The difference between your income and expenses
  • Daily Summary: Shows your income and expenses for the current day

Use the Budget Analysis page to view detailed charts and breakdowns of your spending patterns.
