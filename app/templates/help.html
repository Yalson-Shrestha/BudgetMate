Getting Started

Welcome to BudgetMate! This guide will help you get started with managing your finances effectively.

  • Creating an Account

    Sign up with your email address and create a secure password. Your data is encrypted and protected.

  • Adding Transactions

    Click the "Add Transaction" button on the dashboard to record your income or expenses. Include the amount, category, and optional notes.

  • Viewing Reports

    Use the "Budget Analysis" feature to view detailed charts and insights about your spending patterns.

Managing Transactions

Learn how to effectively manage your financial transactions.

  • Monthly View

    Use the month selector to view transactions from any month. Track your spending patterns over time.

  • Categories

    Organize transactions by categories like Food, Transport, Entertainment, etc. This helps in better budget analysis.

  • Deleting Transactions

    Click the delete icon next to any transaction to remove it. A confirmation prompt will appear to prevent accidental deletions.

Mobile Features

BudgetMate is fully optimized for mobile devices.

  • Install as App

    Click the "Install" button in the navigation bar to add BudgetMate to your home screen for quick access.

  • Offline Access

    Once installed, BudgetMate works offline. Your data syncs automatically when you're back online.

  • Touch-Friendly

    All features are optimized for touch interaction, making it easy to manage your finances on the go.
